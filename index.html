A little about Ben

Hello, my name is Ben. I am from the Bay area and have been here for the past 27 years. I have been off and on in the development world, but more recently my interest for web development has been growing non-stop.

I enjoy what I do. I like being able to create the ideas that come from my mind into interesting sites. My passion for coding started back in middle school when my parents sent me to a web design class in Berkeley. Being able to create ideas with photoshop and put it onto a website was exciting at that age. Just starting to see what the possibilities would be with programming.

I am always looking to expand myself, be it in developing or learning new skills. I feel that as we live our lives, we should see everything as a learning experience and never stop growing.
